Our Discord

Our discord is the hub of our growing community, there are members constantly active with a dedicated admin team and mod team. There is always someone on chatting so it is easy to find someone to hop on with. And even when there isnt, we have events constantly being planned from Raid Runs to PvP matches.

How to join

The discord server, such as is the clan, is a private clan. This is to ensure that we are able to moderate easily who is in the clan, making for a more friendly environment and a better expierience for our members.

Therefore, to be able to join the clan all applicants must direct message the owner "Sunwing#1417" on discord to be vetted before being allows into the clan.
