ABOUT
We are TENET, a growing Destiny clan that focuses mainly on PvE activities. We look to bring new people into the clan where everyone is involved in decision making, using a vote based system. There are constant activities going on from Raids to GM's, and even if there is not an event happening it is super easy to make one and find people for it. The clan has a wide range of members with varying experience in the game, from those who have played right from D1 to those who are just starting, so there is always normally someone who can help with raids, triumphs, challenges, GM's and more.
ONLINE MEMBERS
If you would like to enquire about joining our clan and being part of the team then please enter the form below, simply put your discord name and tag (eg: Examplename#1234) followed by a short description of your experience with Destiny so far and what you are looking from in a clan. Simply click the send button and a message will be sent to one of our verifiers who will then look at your application and message you on discord. If we don’t respond straight away, don’t worry, we will reply usually within a day.
